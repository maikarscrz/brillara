How It Works

Charging:

  • • Place under sunlight for 6–8 hours
  • • Or charge via Type-B port for 2 hours

Using Solight:

  • • Automatically lights up in darkness
  • • Detects motion to activate only when needed
  • • Flashes SOS signal in emergencies
  • • Switches to low-brightness mode to extend battery life

Battery Life:

Up to 72 hours of light on a full charge.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Solight waterproof?

A: Yes! It's weather-resistant and can withstand rain and light storms.

Q: How long does Solight stay lit?

A: Up to 72 hours on low brightness mode after a full solar charge.

Q: Can I use Solight indoors?

A: Yes, it works well indoors. Just make sure to charge it in the sun or via USB.

About Us

Mission

At Brillara, our mission is to provide sustainable, affordable, and energy-efficient lighting solutions that empower communities with limited electricity access.

Vision
