HAVE QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

❓

Is Solight waterproof?

Yes! It's weather-resistant and can withstand rain and light storms, making it perfect for outdoor use.

🔋

How long does Solight stay lit?

Up to 72 hours on low brightness mode after a full solar charge - that's 3 full nights of light!

🏠

Can I use Solight indoors?

Absolutely! It works well indoors. Just remember to charge it in sunlight periodically or via USB.

All Solight products come with a 6-month warranty against manufacturing defects.

About Brillara

Brillara is a passionate student-led startup founded by innovative technopreneurs aiming to make a positive impact. With our flagship product, Solight, we're bringing light, safety, and sustainability to homes, campsites, and communities across the Philippines.
